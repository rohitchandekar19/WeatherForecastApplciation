Below are some points, which will help you to handle the application

  • When you land on the dashboard, if no one city added then you can add the new city by clicking on the '+' button.
  • It will redirect you to the Map Selection Screen, if you long click on the location it will add a marker on the map and will show popup to confirm the same.
  • Once you select 'Yes', new city will be added as bookmark and you will redirect to Home screen
  • Click on the city to check weather conditions, you will redirect to city screen where you xcan get the detail weather report of the day.
  • On the city list, swipe left to delete city from the list.
